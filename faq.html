FAQ

Can I use this service for checking availability of Doctor and his fees without actually booking or scheduling an appointment ?

Although, our aim is to assist you in getting an online appointment done for visit to a doctor, we do not prevent you from looking at the doctors availability and his profile.

Can I make the schedule an online appointment and pay later on ?

We do not provide this facility at the moment. Our aim is to assist you in obtaining a confirmed appointment with a doctor on a selected time slot and date.

Can I make an online booking with my credit card for somebody else ?

Yes.This is allowed.

What are the payment options?

You can pay througt credit/debit card.

When will I get my lab test report?

It depends on the test you book.Tests take different times.As soon as reports are generated it will be available to you.

Can I pay for the tests at the time of sample collection?

No.Because lab tests are pre-booked.Payments should be made online.

Is it necessary to register before making the lab test booking?

Yes.Your booking will be initiated under your registered account.

Can I boook appointment through a phone call?

No.We only provide online services.

How much does it take to make an appointment?

The cost of each appointment will vary from another and it will depend on your health requirements.Once you make an appointment you can directly get an estimate before confirmation.

How to know that my booking is successful?

An email will be sent to you once you done a successful booking.

Is my information are private and confidential?

Yes.We do not pass your information to any other third parties.Patient data is fully secured.
